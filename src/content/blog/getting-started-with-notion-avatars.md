## Pro Tips

Here are some tips to make your avatar stand out:

1. **Keep it simple** - Sometimes less is more. A clean avatar with fewer accessories can be more memorable.

2. **Match your style** - Try to capture something about your real appearance or personality.

3. **Use the flip feature** - Don't forget you can flip your avatar horizontally if the orientation doesn't feel right.

4. **Experiment with backgrounds** - The background color or shape can completely change the vibe of your avatar.

5. **Try the random button** - Feeling adventurous? Hit the dice button and let fate decide!
